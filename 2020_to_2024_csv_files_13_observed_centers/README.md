## CPCB Air Pollution Station CSV Files

The air-pollution CSV files contain 5 years of hourly CPCB data for each station. The data were obtained from the CPCB CCR portal:
https://airquality.cpcb.gov.in/ccr/

### File-year mapping

For each station, the year is identified from the filename suffix:

```text
No suffix  -> 2024
(1)        -> 2023
(2)        -> 2022
(3)        -> 2021
(4)        -> 2020
```
